岗位描述
The Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at Karlstad University, Sweden, has an opening for a post-doctoral research fellow in the field of computer networking. The position is a full time, two year position. The starting date for the position is by mutual agreement within the time span October 1, 2015 and January 1, 2016. The post-doctoral fellow will work in the framework of one or both of the EU H2020 projects NEAT (A New, Evolutive API and Transport-Layer Architecture for the Internet) and MONROE (Measuring Mobile Broadband Networks in Europe). NEAT aims to design, develop and validate a novel transport architecture and system that allows services to be dynamically tailored based on application demands, current network conditions, hardware capabilities or local policies and that supports the integration of new functionality in an evolutionary fashion. A decoupling of the services offered to applications from the underlying network technologies will allow applications to take advantage of new features as they evolve and promote innovations across the protocol stack. MONROE proposes to design, build and operate an open, European-scale, and flexible platform with multi-homing capabilities to run experiments on operational 3G/4G Mobile Broadband (MBB) networks. Key MBB performance parameters affecting user experience will be identified, thus enabling accurate, realistic and meaningful monitoring and assessment of the performance of MBB networks. New innovative services and applications will be explored in collaboration with external users.
